My Life

I am from Peru. My city is Iquitos where the Amazon river and the Nanay river come together. I came to the USA in 2013, and I got my first job as a Front Clerk at Publix in 2014.

I have been looking for a job in technology for 3 years, but my interviewers always told me that I have a gap without working in my career. Then I realized that I needed to update my programming skills, so I took some online courses on EDX, SoloLearn, Coursera, HackerRank, DataCamp. While I was taking my online courses, I took some English classes to improve my communication skills at SP College.
